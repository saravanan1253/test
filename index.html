About Me

My name is Vibhav Pottu, and I am a 9-year-old living in Florida. I was born in Atlanta, Georgia, and have always enjoyed being in the spotlight. Dancing, acting, and entertaining people are my biggest passions. I love how dancing lets me express myself through movement and music, and acting allows me to step into different characters and tell stories. Entertaining others brings me great joy, and I am always looking for new ways to make people smile and laugh.
